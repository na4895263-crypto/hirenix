Refund Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

At HireNix, we stand behind the quality of our service. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a straightforward 7-day refund policy for eligible purchases.

Our Promise: If you're not happy within 7 days of your purchase, we'll give you a full refund — no hassle, no long forms.

1. Subscription Refunds (Pro & Premium)

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 7 days of your initial subscription purchase
  • You have not used more than 20 unique job results from your plan's allowance
  • This is your first refund request on your account
Not eligible for refund:
  • Refund requests made after 7 days of purchase
  • Renewal charges — please cancel before renewal to avoid being charged
  • Accounts found to be in violation of our Terms of Service
  • Free plan — no charges, no refunds applicable

2. Add-on Package Refunds

Add-on packages (100, 200, or 300 unique jobs) are eligible for a refund if:

  • You request a refund within 7 days of purchase
  • You have used fewer than 10 unique jobs from the add-on package

Once more than 10 jobs from an add-on have been accessed, the add-on is considered used and is no longer refundable.

3. How to Request a Refund

1

Email us

Send an email to support@hirenix.org with the subject line: "Refund Request"

2

Include your details

Provide your registered email address, the plan or add-on you purchased, and the reason for your refund request.

3

We review & confirm

We will review your request within 1–2 business days and confirm eligibility by email.

4

Refund processed

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days back to your original payment method.

4. Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. Cancellation stops future billing immediately. You will retain access to your plan's features until the end of the current billing period.

Important: Cancelling your subscription does not automatically trigger a refund. If you wish to cancel and receive a refund, please submit a refund request within 7 days of your purchase as described above.

5. Exceptional Circumstances

We understand that sometimes things happen outside the 7-day window. If you experienced a technical issue on our end, were double-charged, or have an exceptional situation, please contact us at support@hirenix.org and we will review your case individually.

6. Changes to This Policy

HireNix reserves the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be communicated via email to active subscribers. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
