Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

At HireNix ("we", "us", or "our"), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use our website at hirenix.org and our job search services.

Simple version: We collect only what we need to run the service. We never sell your personal data. Ever.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Type What We Collect Why
Account Info Name, email address, password (encrypted) To create and manage your account
Profile Info Job title, skills, location, experience level, visa status To personalize your job results
Usage Data Search history, jobs viewed, clicks To improve recommendations and service quality
Payment Info Subscription plan, transaction ID To manage billing (card details handled by Lemon Squeezy — we never see them)
Technical Data IP address, browser type, device type For security and service performance

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver personalized, scored job results based on your profile
  • Send daily job digest emails (if opted in)
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Improve our job matching algorithm
  • Send important service updates and announcements
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse of the Service

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services We Use

To operate HireNix, we work with the following trusted third-party services:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Supabase Database & user authentication supabase.com/privacy
Lemon Squeezy Payment processing lemonsqueezy.com/privacy
Vercel Web hosting & infrastructure vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy
Resend / SendGrid Email delivery Industry-standard email providers
RapidAPI / JSearch Job data aggregation rapidapi.com/privacy

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and handles data according to their respective terms.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase's encrypted servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted passwords (never stored in plain text)
  • HTTPS encryption on all data transfers
  • Regular security reviews
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data

While we take every precaution, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies

HireNix uses essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to log in.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correct — Update inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete — Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from email digests at any time
  • Export — Request an export of your data in a readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@hirenix.org.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymized usage data may be retained for service improvement purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

HireNix is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will remove it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by email for any significant changes. Continued use of HireNix after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
