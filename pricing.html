Frequently Asked Questions

What is a "unique job"?

A unique job is a fresh, real-time job listing pulled directly from job boards and company career pages — jobs that are new to you and haven't been shown before. Cached jobs are previously searched results shared across users, available instantly at no extra cost.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.

What happens when I use up my unique jobs?

You'll see a notification when you're close to your limit. You can still browse unlimited cached jobs, or purchase an add-on pack to get more unique results instantly.

Do add-ons roll over to next month?

No — add-on packs are valid for the current billing month only. Unused jobs from add-ons do not carry over. Plan your purchases accordingly.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on your first subscription purchase. See our full Refund Policy for details.

Do you support H1B visa candidates?

Absolutely. HireNix flags every job with its H1B sponsorship status — confirmed, likely, unknown, or no sponsorship. Jobs that explicitly reject sponsorship are automatically excluded from your results.
